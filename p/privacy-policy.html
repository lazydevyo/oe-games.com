Privacy Policy

1. What information do we collect, and how long do we keep it?

We access information from Google Play Games Services, including your username, user ID, avatar image, scores, achievements, friends list, and game progress. This data is used to display leaderboards, track achievements, and manage game activities like multiplayer sessions. We also use the Google Drive API to create, edit, and delete game-save data directly on your Google Drive, helping to maintain your game settings and progress across devices.

We collect data through Firebase and Google Analytics, including device information, app usage data, and user interactions within the app. This data helps us understand how users interact with our app and improve its functionality and user experience. We retain this data as long as necessary for these purposes, typically for as long as you use the app and for a period afterward as required by law or business needs.

We do not store or collect data directly on our own servers; it is processed and stored by Google Play Services, Firebase, and Google Analytics. Data accessed through these services is used temporarily during online activities and is not stored by us long-term.

2. Why do we process your information?

We process your information to enhance your gaming experience by displaying leaderboards, tracking achievements, and facilitating multiplayer interactions. This includes using your data during online multiplayer sessions to display player names and avatars to others in the same game room. Additionally, we use data collected through Firebase and Google Analytics to analyze app performance, understand user interactions, and improve app functionality and user experience.

We also use certain information for ad placement tailored to your interests, collected by third-party entities.

We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary to improve your gaming experience and app functionality, such as using Photon PUN to enable online multiplayer connectivity and Firebase for app analytics. The data is used solely within the game and app ecosystem and is not stored long-term.

Additional Information

Firebase and Google Analytics:

We use Firebase and Google Analytics to collect app usage data, including user interactions, device information, and event tracking. This data helps us improve app performance, understand user behavior, and enhance user experience. Firebase and Google Analytics data are stored and processed by Google according to their privacy policies.

Advertisers and Third Parties:

Certain third-party entities collect information about your device, including unique device identifiers. This information may be shared with ad publishers and attribution companies to enhance ad placement tailored to your interests.

Cookies:

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These cookies are used solely for functional and analytical purposes. Your IP address and user agent are shared with Google, along with performance and security metrics, to ensure service quality, generate usage statistics, and detect and address abuse.

Online Multiplayer and In-Game Communication Policy:

We utilize Photon Cloud Services for multiplayer and chat functionality. During gameplay, temporary information such as public IP addresses, approximate location, and Lobby Names (including your in-game player name, Google Play Games username, user ID, and avatar) may be used to facilitate player connections. This information is shared with other players online during multiplayer sessions and is only temporarily collected while you are engaging in online activity within our games.

Data Deletion:

You can request the deletion of your data by visiting our website and navigating to the "Help" section at the top right corner, then clicking on "Request Deletion of Your Data."

Contact Information:

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at play@oe-games.com.
