How to Cancel a Subscription

To cancel the subscription, please follow the steps below


From a Phone:

  1. Open the Google Play app (ensure you are connected to the Google account used for the subscription).
  2. Tap on the profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Select "Payments and subscriptions" from the menu, then choose "Subscriptions."
  4. Locate the "SMX Supermoto Vs Motocross" subscription, tap on it, and then select the "Cancel Subscription" button at the bottom.


From a Computer:

  1. Visit this URL (ensure you are connected to the Google account used for the subscription).
  2. Locate the subscription you want to cancel, click "Manage" and then select "Cancel Subscription".
